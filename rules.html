How to fill up the data?

Definition of Done of how to fill up the data.



Column Name Rules Extra Notes
General title Choose one victim's name (choose person who died over those who were injured), followed by age of the victim in brackets e.g. Junaid Khan (15). If there are more than one victim then append "and others" to the name of the victim e.g. Junaid Khan (15) and others.
menu Choose one from following - Cattle Protection, Crime, Sexual Harassment, Witch Craft, Honour Killing and Other.
sub_crime This is an informal column. Scribble any crime that was committed either by the victim or accused.
date Date when the article was published.
area Ideally enter village or town name. If it is not available, then district name. Do not copy paste the location in the byline since sometime more precise location details are given in the story itself.
state State where the incident occurred.
state_ruling_party Which political party's chief minister was in power in the state when this article was published.
image URL of the cover image
VICTIM - the people who got lynched victim_religion Religion of the victim. If multi-religion then write Hindu and Muslim. If not explicitly mentioned in the article, then leave blank. Start the first word amongst these three columns in capital.
victim_tag Generally the occupation of the victim. E.g. agricultural labourers, farmers, BSF officer, housewife, etc.
victim_gender men, man, boy, boys, women, woman, girl, girls, etc.
victim_action What were the victims doing when the mob lynching took place? End this statement with a full stop.
victim_names Comma separated list of name of victims followed by their age in bracket.
ACCUSED - the people who did the lynching accused_religion Religion of the accused If multi-religion then write Hindu and Muslim. If not explicitly mentioned in the article, then leave blank. Start the first word amongst these three columns in capital.
accused_tag Generally the occupation of the accused E.g. agricultural labourers, farmers, BSF officer, housewife, etc.
accused_gender men, man, boy, boys, women, woman, girl, girls, etc.
accused_action What were the accused doing before the mob lynching took place? End this statement with a full stop.
accused_names Comma separated list of name of victims followed by their age in bracket.
The details of the lynching the_lynching How did the actual lynching take place? More details on the incident.
count_injured Number of victims who did not die but got injured, raped, harrassed, etc.
count_dead Number of victims who died
Were the victims doing something illegal? does_the_state_ criminalise_ victims_actions Value for this column can only be Yes, No or Don't know.
which_law Need to explain in simple words which law did the victim potentially violate



Following columns need to read like a narrative is appended.

{victim_religion} {victim_tag} ({victim_gender}) {victim_action}

Muslim farmers (men) were legally transporting two milch cows to Haryana in a pickup truck.



Were the victims doing something illegal?

Example - Junaid Khan did not do anything illegal. He was simply traveling in train. However there is a case where a couple of Muslim women were transporting bufallo meat in Madhya Pradesh where it is illegal to possess or sell bufallo meat. Hence, the victim's actions can be illegal.
