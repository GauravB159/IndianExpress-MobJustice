#TheLynched

Mobs enacting violent justice on alleged criminals in India.

We analysed 298 incidents of mob justice spanning across 7 years and 27 states.

Here is what we found.



Why #TheLynched?

The national debate on mob lynching has heated up. However, the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) does not record data on lynchings.

With so much going on, we thought it would be prudent to collect data on lynchings from across the country and analyse the reasons that actually lead to murderous behaviour by mobs.

Hence, we scoured IndianExpress.com for first incident reports of mob lynchings from 2010.

Mobs lynch people for all sorts of reasons ranging from Witch Craft to Theft.

If we ignore cases in New Delhi, then ~87% of the lynchings take place in states with:

  • Police to Population ratios that are lower than UN recommendation.
  • Bad or Below Average Judge to Population ratio.
