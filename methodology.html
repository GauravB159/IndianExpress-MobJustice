Methodology


Following data is being collected.

Copy paste from the article

1. *Headline of the article

2. *URL of the article

3. *Byline e.g. Reporter name, Express News Service, PTI, Reuters, etc.

4. *Byline summary. If story_source is "Express Web Desk", "PTI", "IANS"... then story_source_summary is "Wire Copies". If story_source is "Express News Service" then story_source_summary is "Wire with reporter input". If story_source is has a reporters name e.g. Ravik Bhattacharya then story_source_summary is "Reportage".

5. *When was the article published?

6. URL of the Cover Image from the article

Where did it happen?

7. *Where did the incident occur? (State)

8. *District

9. Area. Hint - Go as local as possible e.g. village name, town name.

10. *Area classification. Rural or Urban.

11. *Which partyâ€™s Chief Minister was in power in the state when the incident occurred?

12. *Police to Population Ratio in that state

Data on Police Organizations as on 01.01.2016 compiled by Bureau of Police Research and Development. There are only 2 valid values:

  • Below UN recommendation
  • Above UN recommendation

13. *Judge to Population Ratio in that state

Numbers of Judges for the Subordinate Courts, as on March 31, 2016 (from Court News, publication of the Supreme Court, Vol XI Issue No. 1 Jan - Mar 2016). There are 5 valid values:

  • Good
  • Above Average
  • Average
  • Below Average
  • Bad

14. *Latitude. You will have to find it manually.

15. *Longitude. You will have to find it manually.

What happened?

These columns would be used to automatically construct sentences. Hence, fill accordinly keeping capitalization, punctuation in mind. Example "Unknown truckers (men) were allegedly carrying buffalo calves. Mob beat them up and also reportedly vandalized the vehicles in which the calves were being carried.

Column Name Is Mandatory Description Rules
victim_religion Optional

Take directly from article if mentioned. Deduce from name where possible to ascertain with some surety. In case of confusion, leave blank.

{victim_religion} {victim_tag} ({victim_gender}) {victim_action}. -- will read as a sentence. Hence, the first word amongst victim_religion and victim_tag should be capital. victim_action should end with a full stop.
victim_tag Optional

Generally the occupation of the victim. E.g. agricultural labourers, farmers, BSF officer, housewife, etc.
victim_gender Optional

men, man, boy, boys, women, woman, girl, girls, etc.
victim_action Optional

What were the victims doing when the mob lynching took place? End this statement with a full stop.
accused_religion Optional

Take directly from article if mentioned. Deduce from name where possible to ascertain with some surety. In case of confusion, leave blank.

{accused_religion} {accused_tag} ({accused_gender}) {accused_action}. -- will read as a sentence. Hence, the first word amongst accused_religion and accused_tag should be capital. accused_action should end with a full stop.
accused_tag Optional
accused_gender Optional
accused_action Optional

What were the accused doing before the mob lynching took place? End this statement with a full stop.
the_lynching Optional

How did the actual lynching take place? More details on the incident.

More details

Column Name Is Mandatory Description Rules
count_injured Mandatory

Number of people impacted but not dead.
count_dead Mandatory
victim_names Optional

Comma separated list of name of victims followed by their age in bracket.
title Mandatory

Choose one victim's name (choose person who died over those who were injured), followed by age of the victim in brackets e.g. Junaid Khan (15). If there are more than one victim then append "and others" to the name of the victim e.g. Junaid Khan (15) and others.
how_was_the_lynching_planned Mandatory

3 categories for now: 1. Planned where it was clearly planned against the victim. 2. Sporadic where it was a spontaneous act. 3. Neighbourhood watch where the mob was on the lookout for alleged criminals and prepared to punish him/her/them.
accused_names Optional

Comma separated list of name of accused followed by their age in bracket.
did_the_police_intervene_and_prevent_the_death? Mandatory

Yes if the police intervened and prevented a possible death. Otherwise, no.
