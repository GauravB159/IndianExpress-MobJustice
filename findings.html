#TheLynched

Mobs enacting violent justice on alleged criminals in India.


Mobs lynch people for all sorts of reasons ranging from Witch Craft to Theft.

If we ignore cases in New Delhi, then ~87% of the lynchings take place in states with:

  • Police to Population ratios that are lower than UN recommendation.
  • Bad or Below Average Judge to Population ratio.

Lynchings have been constantly on the rise since 2012.
